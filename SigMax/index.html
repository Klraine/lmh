Influencer Partnership & Campaign Participation Agreement

Terms & Conditions for Influencer Marketing Campaigns

Version 1.0 May 2026 Nairobi, Kenya mort@linkagemediahub.co.ke

1. Introduction

This Influencer Partnership & Campaign Participation Agreement ("Agreement") outlines the terms and conditions governing the working relationship between Linkage Media Hub ("LMH," "Agency," "we," "us," or "our") and the participating Influencer ("you" or "your") for the execution of influencer marketing campaigns, promotions, collaborations, and related digital marketing activities.

By participating in campaigns coordinated by Linkage Media Hub, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agreed to be bound by the terms outlined in this Agreement.

1.1 Purpose of This Agreement

This Agreement exists to establish a professional, organized, and transparent framework between influencers and Linkage Media Hub. The digital space moves quickly, and clear expectations protect both parties while ensuring campaigns are executed efficiently and professionally.

We value long term relationships, reliability, and professionalism over one-off transactions.

1.2 Scope of Services

Linkage Media Hub coordinates influencer campaigns that may include:

  • Sponsored Instagram posts, reels, stories, and live sessions
  • TikTok promotional campaigns
  • Brand awareness campaigns
  • Product or service promotions
  • Event marketing and attendance
  • WhatsApp and Telegram promotional activities
  • Affiliate or referral-based campaigns
  • Ambassador partnerships and long-term collaborations
  • Content creation for client marketing use

Campaign requirements may vary depending on the client, target audience, and campaign objectives.

2. Campaign Participation

2.1 Campaign Selection

Participation in campaigns is entirely at the discretion of Linkage Media Hub and the client involved. Being part of our influencer network does not guarantee automatic campaign opportunities.

Campaign selection may depend on factors including:

  • Audience relevance
  • Engagement quality
  • Professionalism
  • Communication consistency
  • Content quality
  • Reliability during previous collaborations
  • Brand compatibility

Follower count alone does not determine campaign eligibility.

2.2 Campaign Instructions

For each campaign, influencers may receive specific instructions regarding:

  • Posting dates and deadlines
  • Captions or talking points
  • Brand mentions or tags
  • Hashtags
  • Visual requirements
  • Story duration or post retention period
  • Call-to-action requirements

Influencers are expected to follow campaign guidelines reasonably and professionally while maintaining their natural content style and audience authenticity.

2.3 Content Standards

Content submitted or posted as part of LMH campaigns must:

  • Maintain acceptable visual and audio quality
  • Avoid misleading claims about products or services
  • Avoid offensive, discriminatory, or unlawful material
  • Reflect professionalism appropriate for the represented brand
  • Remain publicly accessible for the agreed campaign duration unless otherwise instructed

Our Philosophy: We believe the strongest marketing feels authentic, conversational, and believable rather than forced advertising.

3. Payment Terms

3.1 Payment Structure

Influencer compensation may vary depending on:

  • Campaign size
  • Platform used
  • Audience engagement
  • Deliverable quantity
  • Exclusivity requirements
  • Campaign duration
  • Client budget

Compensation structures may include:

  • Fixed campaign payments
  • Per-post or per-story payments
  • Performance-based incentives
  • Affiliate commissions
  • Long-term ambassador retainers

Specific payment terms for each campaign will be communicated separately.

3.2 Payment Processing

Unless otherwise agreed in writing:

  • Clients remit campaign payments to Linkage Media Hub first
  • LMH then processes influencer payments after confirmation of deliverables and campaign compliance
  • Payments are typically made via M-Pesa, bank transfer, or other agreed methods

This structure ensures campaign coordination, accountability, and smoother project management for all parties involved.

3.3 Payment Eligibility

Influencer payment eligibility is dependent upon:

  • Completion of agreed deliverables
  • Compliance with campaign instructions
  • Timely posting and participation
  • Maintenance of posted content for the agreed duration
  • Submission of requested analytics or proof of posting where applicable

Failure to fulfill campaign obligations may result in delayed, reduced, or withheld payment depending on the nature of the issue.

3.4 Refunds & Reversals

If an influencer:

  • Deletes campaign content prematurely
  • Fails to post within agreed timelines
  • Substantially deviates from campaign instructions
  • Engages in conduct that damages the client's reputation during an active campaign

Linkage Media Hub reserves the right to:

  • Request partial or full repayment
  • Withhold future payments
  • Suspend future collaborations
  • Or terminate the working relationship entirely

Professional reliability matters deeply in marketing partnerships. A single careless post can damage an entire campaign.

4. Content Usage Rights

4.1 Campaign Content Usage

By participating in LMH campaigns, you grant Linkage Media Hub and the associated client a non-exclusive right to repost, share, screenshot, archive, or reference campaign-related content for marketing, portfolio, reporting, or promotional purposes unless otherwise agreed in writing.

This may include:

  • Social media reposts
  • Portfolio showcases
  • Client reports
  • Website displays
  • Promotional graphics featuring campaign results

Ownership of your personal account remains entirely yours.

4.2 Editing & Republishing

LMH and its clients may reasonably resize, crop, or adapt campaign content for cross-platform usage provided such modifications do not intentionally misrepresent the influencer.

5. Professional Conduct

5.1 Communication Expectations

Influencers are expected to maintain reasonable communication throughout active campaigns.

This includes:

  • Responding within reasonable timeframes
  • Communicating delays early
  • Confirming deliverables honestly
  • Maintaining respectful communication with LMH representatives and clients

Disappearing during live campaigns creates operational and reputational risks for all involved parties.

5.2 Reputation & Brand Safety

During active campaigns, influencers are expected to avoid conduct likely to seriously damage the associated client's public reputation.

This includes, but is not limited to:

  • Hate speech
  • Fraudulent activity
  • Deliberate misinformation
  • Public harassment
  • Criminal conduct directly affecting campaign credibility

LMH reserves the right to immediately discontinue collaborations where brand safety concerns arise.

6. Exclusivity & Conflict of Interest

Certain campaigns may involve temporary exclusivity arrangements.

For example, an influencer promoting one restaurant brand may be restricted from promoting direct competitors for a limited agreed period.

Any exclusivity requirements will be communicated before campaign acceptance.

Unless explicitly agreed otherwise, influencers remain free to work with other brands and agencies.

7. Independent Contractor Relationship

Influencers engaged through Linkage Media Hub operate as independent contractors and not as employees, partners, or legal representatives of LMH.

Nothing in this Agreement creates:

  • Employment relationships
  • Joint ventures
  • Agency authority
  • Or ownership interests

Influencers remain responsible for their own taxes, statutory obligations, and account management.

8. Campaign Changes & Cancellations

8.1 Campaign Adjustments

Clients and campaigns may evolve due to market conditions, scheduling changes, or strategic adjustments. LMH reserves the right to reasonably modify campaign timelines, deliverables, or posting schedules where necessary.

We will always attempt to communicate such changes promptly.

8.2 Campaign Cancellation

In rare circumstances, campaigns may be canceled due to:

  • Client withdrawal
  • Budget changes
  • Legal concerns
  • Force majeure events
  • Or unforeseen operational issues

Where work has already been reasonably completed, LMH may determine partial compensation at its discretion depending on the situation.

9. Limitation of Liability

9.1 Platform Issues

Linkage Media Hub is not responsible for:

  • Account restrictions
  • Shadow bans
  • Platform outages
  • Reduced reach
  • Algorithmic changes
  • Or technical failures caused by third-party social media platforms

The digital landscape changes constantly, often without warning.

9.2 Indirect Damages

LMH shall not be liable for indirect losses including:

  • Lost opportunities
  • Lost profits
  • Audience decline
  • Reputational disputes beyond our direct control
  • Or third-party platform enforcement actions

9.3 Maximum Liability

In all circumstances, Linkage Media Hub's maximum liability shall not exceed the amount paid to the influencer for the specific campaign giving rise to the dispute.

10. Termination of Relationship

10.1 Termination Rights

Either party may discontinue the working relationship at any time.

However, active campaign obligations already accepted before termination must still be fulfilled unless otherwise agreed.

10.2 Immediate Suspension

LMH reserves the right to immediately suspend or terminate collaborations in cases involving:

  • Fraud
  • Fake engagement manipulation
  • Breach of campaign confidentiality
  • Severe misconduct
  • Repeated unreliability
  • Or material breach of this Agreement

11. Governing Law & Dispute Resolution

This Agreement shall be governed by and interpreted in accordance with the laws of the Republic of Kenya.

In the event of any dispute arising from this Agreement, both parties agree to first attempt resolution through good-faith communication and negotiation before pursuing formal legal action.

Where necessary, disputes may be submitted to the appropriate courts of Kenya.

12. Agreement Acceptance

By participating in campaigns coordinated by Linkage Media Hub, accepting campaign briefs, receiving campaign materials, or accepting payment for campaign participation, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agreed to the terms outlined in this Agreement.

Professionalism builds reputation. Reputation builds longevity. And in this industry, longevity is worth more than temporary hype.

Client Marketing Services Agreement

Terms & Conditions for Influencer Marketing, Digital Promotion, and Campaign Management Services

Version 1.0 May 2026 Nairobi, Kenya mort@linkagemediahub.co.ke

1. Introduction

This Client Marketing Services Agreement ("Agreement") outlines the terms and conditions governing the relationship between Linkage Media Hub ("LMH," "Agency," "we," "us," or "our") and the Client ("you" or "your") for the provision of influencer marketing, promotional campaigns, digital visibility services, and related marketing coordination activities.

By engaging Linkage Media Hub for services, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agreed to be bound by the terms outlined in this Agreement.

1.1 Purpose of This Agreement

This Agreement exists to establish a professional, transparent, and organized working relationship between Linkage Media Hub and its clients.

Marketing campaigns involve coordination between brands, influencers, content creators, timelines, platforms, and audience behavior. Clear expectations protect both parties and help campaigns operate smoothly and effectively.

Visibility without structure becomes noise. Structure creates results.

1.2 Scope of Services

Linkage Media Hub may provide services including, but not limited to:

  • Influencer sourcing and coordination
  • Instagram, TikTok, Facebook, X, and WhatsApp promotions
  • Brand awareness campaigns
  • Product and service marketing
  • Sponsored content coordination
  • Campaign planning and execution
  • Digital brand positioning
  • Social media visibility campaigns
  • Event promotion campaigns
  • Content creator partnerships
  • Ambassador campaign management
  • Audience engagement campaigns

Campaign details may vary depending on budget, industry, campaign objectives, and platform suitability.

2. Campaign Coordination

2.1 Influencer Selection

Linkage Media Hub carefully selects influencers and creators based on factors including:

  • Audience relevance
  • Engagement quality
  • Brand compatibility
  • Professionalism
  • Geographic relevance
  • Campaign objectives

While client preferences are welcomed, final influencer recommendations remain subject to operational feasibility, availability, and campaign suitability.

Follower count alone is not treated as the sole indicator of influence or campaign effectiveness.

2.2 Campaign Execution

LMH coordinates communication, scheduling, posting timelines, and general campaign management between the client and participating influencers.

Campaign deliverables may include:

  • Posts
  • Stories
  • Reels
  • Videos
  • Mentions
  • Event attendance
  • Product demonstrations
  • Sponsored integrations

Specific deliverables will typically be discussed and agreed upon before campaign launch.

2.3 Creative Flexibility

Influencer marketing performs best when creators are allowed to communicate naturally with their audiences.

While LMH and the client may provide guidance, messaging points, captions, or branding direction, influencers may adapt delivery styles to fit their audience tone and platform culture.

Authenticity generally performs better than rigid scripted advertising.

3. Client Responsibilities

To ensure successful campaign execution, clients are expected to:

  • Provide accurate information regarding products or services
  • Supply logos, visuals, videos, or promotional materials where necessary
  • Communicate campaign goals clearly
  • Respond to approvals and feedback requests within reasonable timeframes
  • Fulfill payment obligations as agreed
  • Maintain professional communication throughout the campaign period

Delays in approvals or required materials may affect campaign timelines and deliverables.

3.1 Product & Service Accuracy

Clients are solely responsible for ensuring that all products, services, claims, pricing, and promotional information provided to LMH are accurate, lawful, and compliant with applicable regulations.

LMH does not independently verify all client claims and is not responsible for misinformation originating from the client.

4. Payment Terms

4.1 Campaign Fees

Campaign pricing may vary depending on:

  • Influencer size and reach
  • Campaign complexity
  • Deliverable quantity
  • Platform selection
  • Campaign duration
  • Production requirements
  • Urgency and scheduling demands

All pricing discussions, quotations, or proposals provided by LMH remain confidential unless otherwise agreed.

4.2 Payment Structure

Unless otherwise agreed in writing:

  • Campaign payments are typically made upfront before campaign launch
  • Partial upfront deposits may apply for larger campaigns
  • Campaign execution may begin only after confirmation of required payment

This structure allows LMH to secure influencer participation, allocate resources, and coordinate campaign logistics effectively.

4.3 Non-Refundable Operational Costs

Once campaign coordination has begun, certain operational costs become immediately active.

These may include:

  • Influencer booking commitments
  • Content preparation
  • Scheduling allocations
  • Campaign management time
  • Creative planning

As a result, portions of campaign payments may become non-refundable once campaign preparations are underway.

4.4 Late Payments

Late payments may result in:

  • Campaign delays
  • Paused deliverables
  • Postponed influencer scheduling
  • Suspension of services
  • Or cancellation of ongoing coordination activities

Repeated payment delays may affect future collaboration eligibility.

5. Influencer Participation & Performance

5.1 Third-Party Creators

Influencers and creators participating in campaigns are independent third parties and not employees of Linkage Media Hub.

While LMH coordinates campaigns professionally, we cannot guarantee:

  • Exact sales figures
  • Viral performance
  • Audience purchasing behavior
  • Platform algorithm reach
  • Or specific engagement outcomes

Marketing improves probability. It does not control human behavior.

5.2 Performance Variability

Campaign results may vary due to factors including:

  • Audience behavior
  • Market trends
  • Timing
  • Platform algorithms
  • Economic conditions
  • Competitor activity
  • And content reception

No campaign outcome can be guaranteed with absolute certainty.

5.3 Influencer Conduct

LMH works to maintain professional standards among creators. However, influencers remain independent individuals with their own personalities, audiences, and online presence.

LMH is not responsible for unrelated statements, opinions, or personal conduct expressed by influencers outside the scope of campaign deliverables.

6. Content Usage & Promotion Rights

6.1 Campaign Content Usage

LMH reserves the right to repost, showcase, archive, reference, or include campaign-related materials in:

  • Portfolios
  • Presentations
  • Social media pages
  • Marketing materials
  • Case studies
  • Or internal reporting documentation

This may include screenshots, campaign visuals, analytics, or public campaign content.

6.2 Brand References

By engaging LMH services, clients grant permission for Linkage Media Hub to reference the completed collaboration as part of our professional portfolio unless otherwise agreed in writing.

We believe successful work should be visible.

7. Campaign Delays, Adjustments & Cancellations

7.1 Scheduling Adjustments

Campaign schedules may occasionally shift due to:

  • Influencer availability
  • Platform issues
  • Content revisions
  • Public events
  • Technical problems
  • Or strategic campaign adjustments

LMH will make reasonable efforts to communicate schedule changes promptly.

7.2 Campaign Cancellation

Clients may request campaign cancellation before launch.

However, if campaign coordination, influencer booking, or production activities have already begun, cancellation fees or partial payment retention may apply to cover operational commitments already made.

7.3 Force Majeure

LMH shall not be held liable for delays or failures caused by circumstances beyond reasonable control, including:

  • Internet outages
  • Platform disruptions
  • Governmental actions
  • Strikes
  • Natural disasters
  • Cyber incidents
  • Or widespread technical failures

The digital world is powerful, but not invincible.

8. Limitation of Liability

8.1 Platform Dependency

Social media platforms are controlled by third-party companies whose systems, algorithms, policies, and enforcement actions remain outside LMH's control.

We are not liable for:

  • Reduced reach
  • Shadow bans
  • Account restrictions
  • Platform outages
  • Content removals
  • Or algorithmic suppression

8.2 Indirect Losses

LMH shall not be liable for indirect or consequential losses including:

  • Loss of profits
  • Loss of business opportunities
  • Reputational disputes
  • Audience reactions
  • Or market performance fluctuations

8.3 Maximum Liability

In all cases, Linkage Media Hub's total liability shall not exceed the amount paid by the client for the specific campaign giving rise to the dispute.

9. Confidentiality

Both parties agree to maintain reasonable confidentiality regarding:

  • Campaign pricing
  • Influencer payment structures
  • Internal strategy discussions
  • Private campaign analytics
  • And non-public business information

Professional trust matters in long-term partnerships.

10. Service Termination

10.1 Termination Rights

Either party may discontinue the working relationship at any time.

However, ongoing campaigns already approved or launched may still require settlement of operational costs and outstanding obligations.

10.2 Suspension of Services

LMH reserves the right to suspend or terminate services in cases involving:

  • Non-payment
  • Abusive conduct
  • Fraudulent activity
  • Unlawful products or services
  • Reputational risks
  • Or material breach of this Agreement

Not every opportunity is worth attaching a reputation to.

11. Governing Law & Dispute Resolution

This Agreement shall be governed by and interpreted in accordance with the laws of the Republic of Kenya.

In the event of any dispute arising from this Agreement, both parties agree to first attempt resolution through good-faith negotiation before pursuing formal legal proceedings.

Where necessary, disputes may be submitted to the appropriate courts of Kenya.

12. Agreement Acceptance

By engaging Linkage Media Hub services, approving campaign proposals, making campaign payments, or participating in coordinated campaigns, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agreed to the terms outlined in this Agreement.

Marketing is not merely about visibility. It is about perception, timing, trust, and coordination. The brands that understand this rarely remain invisible for long.
